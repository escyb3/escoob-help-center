📖 Help Articles

How do I reset my password?

Click on “Forgot Password” on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to you.

How to contact technical support?

You can use the “📨 Contact Us” or “🛠️ Support” buttons on the home page. Fill in your issue and we’ll get back to you.

Can I use the chat in multiple languages?

Yes! The Escoob AI Chat understands and responds in many languages automatically.

How to update my email address?

Log in, go to your profile settings, and update your email from there.
